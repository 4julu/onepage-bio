Interests

I like to build things, ride my bike, and listen to music.

In terms of "building things," I cast a pretty wide net to include crafts, gardening, cooking, software, and hardware -- in my mind, it's all one mixed-up jumble of "what are my current projects?" My idea of a good Sunday is to hole up in a woodshop for most of the day, pop by the farmer's market for groceries, then stay up late wiring up a Raspberry Pi to do ridiculous things to the cat.

I'm currently most interested in these technologies:

  • Deep learning. It's an exciting time for machine learning. I'm working through several online classes, and swap articles with collegues.
  • Rapid phyiscal prototyping. I have a Glowforge laser cutter (I was a minor angel investor) and I'm obsessed by the potential of in-home precision tooling.
  • AR/VR. While I realize the "gold rush" of investment in VR media is drying up, I think there's real industrial and business potential in AR/VR beyond entertainment. I'm spending time on Unity classes and my own Vive projects.
